SAFE REFUGE
Mission

At Safe Refuge, we are dedicated to creating awareness on Sexual and Gender-Based Violence (SGBV) and providing support for survivors worldwide. Our mission is to raise awareness, empower survivors, and advocate for systemic change to create a safer and more equitable society for all.

Our Services

We offer a range of services and resources to support survivors of Sexual and Gender-based Violence

  • Confidential counseling services provided by trained professionals
  • Legal assistance and advocacy for survivors navigating the justice system
  • Educational materials on consent, healthy relationships, and survivor empowerment
  • Support groups and online communities for survivors to connect and share their experiences
Prevention Measures
Education and Awareness

Promote education on topics such as consent, healthy relationships, gender equality, and respect for diversity. This education should start at an early age and continue throughout life, reaching diverse populations through schools, community programs, and media campaigns.

Legal and Policy
Reform

Advocate for laws and policies that criminalize SGBV, protect survivors, and promote gender equality. This includes ensuring access to justice for survivors, holding perpetrators accountable, and addressing gaps in legal frameworks.

Changing Social Norms

Challenge harmful gender norms and stereotypes that perpetuate SGBV, such as expectations of masculinity and femininity. Encourage positive masculinity and promote the idea that all individuals deserve respect and autonomy regardless of gender.

Empowerment of Women and Girls

Addressing the root causes of SGBV often involves empowering women and girls by promoting their rights, access to education, economic opportunities, and leadership roles in society.
