SAFE REFUGE

Empowering Survivors

Safe Refuge offers a comprehensive awareness and support system for victims of sexual and gender based violence.

Core Services

Our specialized services offer immediate assistance, ongoing support and community education to empower survivors and prevents future incidents of violence.

Emergency Aid

Rapid response and support for immediate survivors needs, including shelter, healthcare and safety planning.

Legal Help

Assistance navigating legal matters, from obtaining restraining orders to court representation.

Therapy

Licensed professionals providing trauma-informed counselling for healing and recovery.

Prevention Measures
Education and Awareness

Promote education on topics such as consent, gender equality.

Empowerment of Females

Adressing the root causes of SGBV often involve empowering women and girls.

Changing Social Norms

Challenge harmful gender norms and stereotypes that perpetuate SGBV.

Survivor stories

Maria Samuel

Survivor

Maria, a refugee fleeing conflict in her home country, experienced sexual exploitation and violence during her journey to safety. Despite the trauma she endured, Maria found support from humanitarian organizations providing services for survivors of SGBV.

Maya Jacob

Survivor

Maya was a college student who was sexually assaulted by a classmate at a party. Despite feeling ashamed and scared, Maya decided to seek help from a counselor on campus. With support from friends, family, and professional help, Maya went through therapy and legal proceedings.

Ahmed Abubakar

Survivor

Ahmed was trapped in a marriage defined by violence and control. Despite the stigma, he broke his silence and sought help, With our assistance, he found the strength to leave his abusive spouse and start anew, proving that SGBV can affect anyone regardless of gender.
